Orbit AdServer - Your Own Ad Serving Software

Orbit AdServer is designed to operate at the back-end of your websites creating a robust platform your advertisers will find easy-to-use.

Orbit AdServer system offers a complete solution for creating and managing advertising campaigns. Using Orbit AdServer gives you the ability to publish advertisements on any of the websites you control; easily converting your web traffic into ad revenue. Advertisers registered on your system, can create and manage their marketing campaigns.

Orbit AdServer offers:

  • Serving text, image, flash advertisements
  • CPC, CPM, CPA, Flat Rate cost models
  • Countries and languages targeting
  • Detailed analytics and data-rich reports
  • Zoning on a site
  • Contextual and keyword-based advertising
  • Visitor security methods for avoiding fraudulent clicks or undesirable visitors
  • and many other useful features.
