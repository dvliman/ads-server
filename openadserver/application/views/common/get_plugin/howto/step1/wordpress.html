1. Upload installation package by clicking on the “Download” button in the bottom screen area.
  2. Unpack the archive to the directory wp-content/plugins/ of your website.
  3. Now you need to activate the Plug-in, for this you need to go to the administrative interface of your blog.
  4. In the left menu select “Plugins” item. Click on the “Inactive” link in the top area of your screen.
  5. In the list of available plug-in find ”<%SITE_NAME%> Ads”. Click on the “Activate” link.

That’s all the plug-in is successfully installed and activated. Now you can move to adjustment and display of ads.
